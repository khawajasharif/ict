Buy Hardware Directly From AryanIct, Lifetime Warranty Included!

Whether you’re planning to set up a new gaming server rig, an office dev server, or a production hosting server, normally the hardest part is determining the correct hardware to get your specific job done.

Questions will arise. What kind of server do I need? Do you need a single CPU or would two be better? Will this chassis support the motherboard I just ordered? Do I need a raid card? Will this RAM even work?

With so many hardware options available, ordering and building a server properly is challenging for novices and experts alike. For this reason, we here at AryanIct.com offer an expert service to do this for you. When you purchase the hardware from us you save time in costly mistakes and time in provisioning the server. We do all that for you and is inclusive of our hardware pricing.

Being in the industry since 2006, AryanIct.com has developed some long lasting relationships with major top tier hardware providers which allow us to get the latest and greatest hardware available at affordable rates. The bulk discounts we receive means savings for you.

The 8 plus years that we’ve been doing this has given us a lot of valuable knowledge. We test an incredibly expansive array of hardware from different CPUs, motherboards, RAM sticks, power supply units, and more. This extensive testing and experience narrows our offerings to a select group of hardware that is most compatible and powerful when used together, while also being cost effective. Sometimes the most expensive isn’t always the best.

Apart from the assurance that the hardware you’re ordering has been expertly chosen and assembled, we offer something very exclusive to our clients you’ll also receive the peace of mind that the hardware you’re purchasing will receive FULL lifetime warranty while hosted or colocated within the AryanIct facility.

Once we build your fully managed, customized server, it will reside in our 25,000 square foot, state-of-the-art enterprise datacenter. You’ll receive round-the-clock full service and support from our NOC Technicians, System Administrators, and Abuse and Security Teams.

Say one or two years down the line, a drive in your server fails. AryanIct.com will replace it with an equivalent or better hard drive. Not only that but we will provide the labor necessary to replace the hard drive at no cost to you.

Did your server drive fail? If so, we will also re-image your data (if possible) to the new drives from the failing drive. All hardware management (file system checks, drive failure detection, linux system re-imaging) will always be free of cost when you purchase your hardware directly from AryanIct.com. No other vendor will give you free labor and replacement parts on site!

Fun Fact: AryanIct.com has engineered, built, and provisioned over 5,000 servers and 95% of the servers in our facilities have been built by us using top tier level hardware from the likes of Seagate, Supermicro, Intel, LSI, and many more.

Buying hardware from AryanIct.com and using our colocation services gives you peace of mind on all parts and labor thanks to the lifetime warranty. Should anything fail on the hardware you purchased from us, it’s on us to fix it free of charge. Never worry about RMA’s or paying remote hands to check things, or having to worry about having to order a new part. When things go wrong, you will be prepared!

If you’re looking to make a worthwhile investment for your company by purchasing your own hardware over leasing, trust in AryanIct.com. We will assist you with configuring a system to fit your needs. If you have any further questions, please contact our Dedicated Service Sales Team and we will be happy to get any questions answered as soon as possible.
