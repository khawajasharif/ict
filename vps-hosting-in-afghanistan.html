VPS Hosting FAQ

A Virtual Private Server (VPS) is a partitioned portion of a physical server, functioning as an independent virtual machine with dedicated resources. VPS hosting, provided by companies like AryanIct.com, enables users to host websites and various software applications on these virtual servers. Offering more power and control than shared hosting, VPS allows for customization and flexibility.

VPS features its own Operating System (OS) and can be separately rebooted, ensuring isolation and non-interference between different servers. While AryanIct.com manages the physical servers and guarantees speed, uptime, and stability, users are responsible for managing their individual servers, creating an ideal balance of power and support.

VPS hosting is used for a variety of online projects that require more power, control, and flexibility, such as robust websites, web apps, and online game servers. It provides dedicated resources like CPU power, RAM, and disk space, which aren’t shared with other users like in shared hosting, creating an optimal environment for your projects.

Users with a VPS typically have full root access allowing them to install any supported operating systems and configure their servers to their needs. Linux VPS, for example, offers extensive features and controls due to its open-source nature.

VPS hosting offers a balance of resources and cost, making it an ideal choice for those needing more resources than shared hosting can offer but without the higher price tag of a dedicated server.

Selecting AryanIct.com for VPS hosting offers numerous advantages. AryanIct.com’s global data centers ensure your data is closer to your audience, reducing latency and enhancing user experience—an essential aspect of SEO optimization.

Our service is ideal for varied users—from developers and bloggers to e-commerce owners—offering affordable, secure, and scalable VPS plans.

Performance-wise, our powerful CPUs provide superior execution, making each vCPU Core more potent than those from many competitors.

Plus, our top-rated support team is available 24/7/365 local support in Afghanistan, providing responses from experienced engineers in under 15 minutes, ensuring your hosting needs are always taken care of.

Shared hosting is ideal for small projects, where users share server resources and space, limiting customization and potentially impacting performance due to other users’ activities. On the other hand, VPS hosting, like AryanIct.com's offerings, reserves dedicated resources on a physical server for each user, offering more power and customization.

This makes VPS hosting more scalable and powerful than shared hosting, albeit at a higher cost. In shared hosting, you share the server directly, whereas in VPS, you have more control over the virtual space, creating a balance between shared and dedicated server hosting.

VPS hosting provides a balance between shared hosting and dedicated server hosting. Unlike shared hosting, you have greater control over your virtual space in a VPS, yet you still share resources with other users. This makes VPS hosting more powerful than shared hosting but less expensive than a dedicated server. VPS is ideal for moderately-sized web projects, with the added advantage of not having to worry about hardware management.

On the other hand, a dedicated server gives you exclusive access to all the resources, making it the most powerful and flexible option, though at a higher cost. If you want to manage the hardware yourself, build complex architectures, create a private network, or deploy complex non-web solutions, a dedicated server is recommended. Generally, users with a growing volume of web activity upgrade to dedicated servers or Public Cloud solutions for more elaborate, adaptable infrastructures that can accommodate high growth levels.
